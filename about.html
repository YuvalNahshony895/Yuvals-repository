About Broadway Stores, Inc.

Broadway Stores, Inc., formerly known as Broadway-Hale Stores (1950–1974) and Carter Hawley Hale Stores (1974–1994), was a prominent entity in the retail industry.

Type: Public

Traded as: NYSE: BWY

Industry: Retail

Founded: February 24, 1896; 127 years ago as The Broadway in Los Angeles

Founder: Arthur Letts, Sr.

Defunct: October 12, 1995; 28 years ago

Fate: The company was sold to Federated Department Stores, with Macy's emerging as its successor.

Headquarters: Los Angeles, California, US

Area served: United States

Key people: Edward W. Carter, Prentis C. Hale, Philip M. Hawley

Divisions: The Broadway (1950–1995), Weinstock's (1950–1995), Hale's (1950–1967), Wanamaker's (1978-1987), Emporium-Capwell (1970–1995)
